Ramen

A nice bowl of ramen noodles

This ramen recipe is a great recipe if your in a hurry; it can be whipped up in under 5 minutes, and can be enjoyed plenty of ways. Ill cover the most basic way to make ramen noodles, but you can alter the recipe however you like; this is simply the foundation for the recipe.

    Ingredients

  • One packet of ramen noodles (make sure they are healthy ones, not the generic brand)

    Directions

  1. Boil water

  2. Put ramen noodles in once boiling

  3. Cook ramen noodles for 3 minutes

  4. Strain ramen, put into bowl, then add soy sauce and chosen seasoning

  5. Enjoy
