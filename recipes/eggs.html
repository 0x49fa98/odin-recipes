Eggs

Some yummy eggs on toast

This yummy recipe of eggs (with the optional toast) is known worldwide for being an easy to make recipe, while also not taking too much time and energy to make. You can make this delicious meal in ~5 minutes, which makes for a wonderful morning meal.

    Ingredients

  • One or two organic eggs

  • Butter

  • Salt and pepper (optional)

  • One or two pieces of toast

    Directions

  1. Put butter into hot pan, spread evenly and wait to melt almost fully

  2. Crack egg(s) onto pan

  3. Wait for eggs to cook sufficently, then flip over. IMPORTANT: Make sure egg yolk stays intact.

  4. Put salt and pepper on eggs if desired

  5. While eggs are frying, put toast in toaster or airfryer until done

  6. When eggs are done cooking, pick up eggs with spatula and put onto toast

  7. Enjoy
