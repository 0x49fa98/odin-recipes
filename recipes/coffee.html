A wonderful cup of dark coffee

This is a simple recipe for a cup of dark coffee, a healthy and quick meal to start your day.

    Ingredients

  • Dark coffee beans

  • Sugar

  • Heavy cream or milk

    Directions

  1. Grind coffee

  2. Pour water

  3. Soak and stir

  4. Brew

  5. Pour

  6. Add desired amount of sugar and cream/milk

  7. Enjoy
